1. The upper left subwindow shows a series commits made to this
repository. The most recent commit is on the top. Also, we can 
find out the mergepoint.
2. The upper middle subwindow shows the commit authors.
3. The upper right subwindow shows when the commits are made.
4. The lower left subwindow shows some details about the commit,
including its parent, child and branches. Also, we can select to show
the diff, old version or new version of files.
5. The lower right subwindow lists all files influenced by the commit
we concern (merging).
